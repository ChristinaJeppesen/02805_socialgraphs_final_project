She gained notoriety for her on-air spats with celebrities and was the subject of the 2006 [[VH1]] reality television series ''[[The Wendy Williams Experience]],'' which broadcast events surrounding her radio show.\n\nWilliams' other endeavors include authoring several books, appearances in various films and television shows, touring her comedy show,<ref name=\":0\" /><ref>{{Cite web|last=Brady|first=Louisa|title=Wendy Williams Returns to LA in LIPSHTICK - THE PERFECT SHADE OF STAND UP on 10/31011/1|url=https://www.broadwayworld.com/article/Wendy-Williams-Returns-to-LA-in-LIPSHTICK-THE-PERFECT-SHADE-OF-STAND-UP-on-10310111-20140819|access-date=2022-01-14|website=BroadwayWorld.com|language=en}}</ref> and her own product lines, including a fashion line, a jewelry collection and a wig line. Williams was inducted into the [[National Radio Hall of Fame]] in 2009.
