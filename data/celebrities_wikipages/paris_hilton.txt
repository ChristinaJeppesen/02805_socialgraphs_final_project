The socialite, reality star and DJ through the years|url=https://www.usatoday.com/picture-gallery/entertainment/celebrities/2021/02/17/paris-hilton-through-the-years/6761663002/|date=February 17, 2021|newspaper=USA Today}}</ref> is an American media personality, businesswoman, model, entertainer, and socialite. Born in New York City, and raised there and in [[Beverly Hills]], California, she is a great-granddaughter of [[Conrad Hilton]], the founder of [[Hilton Hotels]]. Hilton first attracted [[Tabloid journalism|tabloid]] attention in the late 1990s, when she became a fixture in NYC's social scene, and ventured into modeling at age 19, signing with [[Donald Trump]]'s agency [[Trump Model Management]]. After [[David LaChapelle]] photographed her and sister [[Nicky Hilton|Nicky]] for the September 2000 issue of ''[[Vanity Fair (magazine)|Vanity Fair]]'', Hilton was proclaimed \"New York's leading [[It Girl]]\" in 2001.<ref name=\"biography.com\">{{cite web|url=http://www.biography.com/people/paris-hilton-11271420?page=1|title=Paris Hilton Biography|publisher=Biography.com|access-date=August 10, 2012}}</ref> The reality television series ''[[The Simple Life]]'' (2003\u20132007), in which she starred with her friend [[Nicole Richie]], and a leaked 2001 [[Celebrity sex tape|sex tape]] with her then-boyfriend [[Rick Salomon]], later released as ''[[1 Night in Paris]]'' (2004), catapulted her into global fame.\n\nHilton published her debut book, ''[[Confessions of an Heiress]]'' (2004), which became a [[The New York Times Best Seller list|''New York Times'' Best Seller]], landed her first major film role in the horror remake ''[[House of Wax (2005 film)|House of Wax]]'' (2005), and released her eponymous debut studio album, ''[[Paris (Paris Hilton album)|Paris]]'' (2006), which reached number six on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] and respectively produced the successful single \"[[Stars Are Blind]]\". She has since starred in further [[List of Paris Hilton screen appearances|films and television series]], recorded a line of [[Paris Hilton discography|standalone singles]], and performed as a [[disc jockey]].<ref name=\"time.com\">{{cite news|url=http://time.com/4964680/dj-paris-hilton-ibiza/|title=Here's What Happened When We Went Clubbing with DJ Paris Hilton|magazine=Time}}</ref> Her  media ventures include the reality television series ''[[Paris Hilton's My New BFF]]'' (2008\u20132009), ''[[The World According to Paris]]'' (2011), ''[[Hollywood Love Story]]'' (2018), ''[[Cooking with Paris]]'' (2021), and ''[[Paris in Love]]'' (2021\u20132022); the documentaries ''[[Paris, Not France]]'' (2008), ''[[The American Meme]]'' (2018), and ''[[This Is Paris]]'' (2020); as well as her podcast, ''This is Paris'' (2021\u2013present), on [[iHeartRadio]].<ref>{{cite web|title=Paris Hilton Launching This Is Paris Podcast to Help Her 'Further Connect' with Fans|url=https://people.com/tv/paris-hilton-launching-podcast-iheartradio-this-is-paris/|access-date=May 8, 2021|website=People|language=en}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|title=Paris Hilton's 'This Is Paris' Podcast Completely Diverges From Her YouTube Documentary|url=https://www.bustle.com/entertainment/paris-hilton-launches-this-is-paris-podcast|access-date=May 8, 2021|website=Bustle|language=en}}</ref>\n\nA polarizing and often derided celebrity, Hilton is credited with influencing the revival of the \"[[famous for being famous]]\" phenomenon throughout the 2000s,<ref name=\"phfame\">{{cite web|url=http://www.scoreboard-media.com/paris-hilton-rule/ |title=The Paris Hilton Rule: Famous For Being Famous |work=Scoreboard Media Group |access-date=September 13, 2014 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120528065137/http://www.scoreboard-media.com/paris-hilton-rule/ |archive-date=May 28, 2012 }}</ref> and was, for a number of years, one of the world's most ubiquitous public figures. Critics indeed suggest that she exemplifies the [[celebutante]]\u2014a household name not through talent or work, but through inherited wealth and lavish lifestyle.  ''[[Forbes]]'' included her in its [[Forbes Celebrity 100|Celebrity 100 highest-paid celebrities]] in 2004, 2005, and 2006, and ranked her as the most \"overexposed\" celebrity in 2006 and 2008. Hilton has parlayed her media fame into numerous business endeavours. Under her company, she has produced content for broadcast media, launched a variety of product lines, and opened a chain of self-branded  boutiques worldwide as well as an [[Azure Beach Club Paris Hilton|urban beach club]] in the [[Philippines]].
